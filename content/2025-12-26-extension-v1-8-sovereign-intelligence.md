Today we’re pushing **v1.8** of the Studio OS Browser Extension. 

It marks a fundamental shift in how the OS interacts with your attention. We are moving from **Passive Logging** (recording what happened) to **Sovereign Intelligence** (understanding why it’s happening, as it happens).

## Beyond the Log: Ambient Intent

The browser is the most frequent interface for high-velocity research, yet most "copilots" only wake up when you click a button. v1.8 introduces the **7-Tier Hover Stack**.

The OS now parses your cursor behavior—fixations, saccades, and "hover-dwell"—to determine your ambient intent. 

*   **Epistemic Curiosity**: The system now identifies when your hover suggests a "Deep Read" is necessary and pre-fetches context before you even click.
*   **Signal Nexus**: Every hover is cross-referenced against your existing Knowledge Graph, highlighting resonances with past research in real-time.
*   **System 3 Audit**: If the OS detects aimless scrolling or attention-fragmentation, it triggers a philosophical intervention to help you regain focus.

## On-Demand Product Synthesis

We’ve also added a dedicated **PRODUCT** button and a "Synthesize Sovereign Product" context menu. 

With one click, the **Reporter Agent** audits your raw telemetry and decision ledger to produce a premium-grade deliverable. This isn’t just a summary; it’s a strategic brief, complete with Mermaid diagrams of your knowledge flow and a **Felt Right Index (FRI)** score for the session.

## Does This Feel Right?

The goal of Studio OS isn't to automate your thinking, but to provide a **Situational Cockpit** for it. By making the ambient signals of research visible and structured, we allow for a higher level of sovereignty over our own digital artifacts.

Download v1.8 now and let your OS start watching your intent, not just your clicks.
