I fired the interns. Now, I am looking for the experts.

To build a studio that operates at the speed of thought, we need more than just "chatbots". We need a specialized team of capabilities. Here is the hierarchy of value in the AI era, and the team we are building.

---

## 🥇 1. AI Research Engineer / AI Systems Engineer (Frontier Labs)
*OpenAI, Anthropic, xAI, DeepMind, Mistral*

**Role:** Research + training + optimization for frontier-class models.

**Why it pays the most:**
Because these engineers directly improve model performance, which is worth billions.

**The Studio Fit:**
We need the capability to fine-tune and optimize our own small models, not just consume APIs.

---

## 🥈 2. AI Infrastructure / ML Platform Engineer
*OpenAI, Anthropic, Netflix ML Infra, Amazon AGI Infra*

**Role:**
They build:
*   Inference runtimes
*   GPU orchestration
*   Distributed systems
*   Autoscaling clusters
*   Serving pipelines

**Why it pays:**
AI inference is the new cloud business. These engineers keep the models running globally.

**The Studio Fit:**
This is the "Engine" in our "Engine and Gallery" philosophy. We need robust, local-first inference that scales.

---

## 🥉 3. Principal / Staff Software Engineer
*Google, Meta, Amazon, Cloudflare*

**Role:**
These engineers own:
*   Distributed caching
*   Cloud infra
*   Large-scale backend systems
*   Low-latency networking
*   Database performance

**Why it pays:**
Because they keep billion-user systems running.

---

## 4. Quantitative Researcher / Quant Developer
*Jane Street, Two Sigma, Citadel, HRT*

**Role:**
Algorithmic improvements that drive efficiency.

**The Studio Fit:**
Metric-driven content optimization. We don't just guess; we measure.

---

## 5. Robotics / Autonomous Systems Engineer
*Tesla, Rivian, Waymo, Anduril, Boston Dynamics*

**Role:**
High risk + high complexity.

**The Studio Fit:**
Agents that can take *action* in the real world (or at least, the browser), not just generate text.

---

## 6. Security Engineer / Staff Security Architect
*Google, Meta, Stripe, Coinbase*

**Role:**
Critical protection of high-value assets.

**The Studio Fit:**
Ensuring our "Brain" (knowledge base) remains private and secure.

---

## 7. Engineering Manager (AI or Cloud)
*OpenAI, Nvidia, Meta, Google*

**Role:**
Orchestration and leadership.

**The Studio Fit:**
The "Boss" I installed to manage the workflow.

---

## 8. AR/VR Systems Engineer
*Apple, Meta Reality Labs*

**Role:**
Spatial computing and immersion.

**The Studio Fit:**
Future interfaces for the Gallery.

---

## 9. GPU Systems & Kernel Engineer
*Nvidia*

**Role:**
Low-level hardware optimization.

---

## 10. AI Product Engineer (High-end Startups)
**Role:**
Building the application layer.

**The Studio Fit:**
*This is the category where we sit today.* But to build the future, we must expand upward.
