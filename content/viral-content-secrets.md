I spent the last few hours studying how multi-million dollar startups like n8n and Blotato approach content creation.

What I found changed everything about how I think about posting content online.

## The 1→N Principle

Stop creating content. Start **repurposing** content.

One blog post should become:
- A TikTok video
- An Instagram carousel
- A Twitter thread
- A LinkedIn post
- A YouTube Short

The effort goes into the **thinking**, not the reformatting.

## Viral Hooks Are a Science

Turns out there are patterns. Successful hooks usually fall into five categories:

1.  **Controversy** - Challenge the status quo or popular opinion.
    *   *"I hate [popular tool] and here's why..."*
    *   *"Unpopular opinion: You don't need to post daily to grow."*
2.  **Curiosity** - Tease a secret or counter-intuitive fact.
    *   *"Why is no one talking about X?"*
    *   *"Here's the secret [Experts] won't tell you."*
3.  **Shock** - Start with an extreme statement or visual.
    *   *"I just lost $1,000 in 5 minutes..."*
    *   *"This video will change how you see [Topic] forever."*
4.  **Story** - Personal transformation or journey.
    *   *"How I went from 0 to 10k followers in 30 days."*
    *   *"I was ready to quit until I found this..."*
5.  **Listicle** - Promise specific value/ROI.
    *   *"5 Tools that feel illegal to know."*
    *   *"3 Mistakes killing your growth."*

Pick one. Stick to it. Test which works for your audience.

## The Pipeline Mindset (1 → N Principle)

The creators winning right now aren't working harder—they're working smarter using the **1-to-N Principle**.
*   **1 Core Asset** (e.g., Deep-dive Newsletter or Video)
*   **N Derivative Assets** (Twitter threads, LinkedIn carousels, Shorts)

They have **systems**:
-   **Content Audit:** Identify evergreen high-performers to repost.
-   **Atomization:** Break one video into 5 clips, 3 quotes, and a thread.
-   **Score hooks** before posting using AI.
-   **Iterate** based on data (CTR, retention).

## What I Built

I took these lessons and built my own content pipeline:
- A **Viral Coach** that scores my scripts before I post
- A **Content Repurposer** that turns one article into five formats
- **Workflow Templates** for different content styles

The result? 10x the content output. Same time investment.
