### The Concept
The Graph is an experiment in **navigating complexity**.
Most knowledge management systems are lists or hierarchies. But thoughts used to be associative.
This system visualizes the "semantic distance" between concepts, allowing me to see where ideas cluster and where gaps exist.

### As Infrastructure
It is not just a visualization. It is the underlying data structure of the studio's brain.
Every note, every code snippet, and every decision is a node.
