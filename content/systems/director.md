### The Problem
Most "productivity" is just manual labor disguised as organization. We build todo lists, we manage calendars, we move data from one tab to another.
True leverage requires **automation of agency**, not just automation of tasks.

### The System
**The Director** is a multi-agent operating system designed to run the operational layer of my creative work.
It is not a script. It is an organization.

- **The Librarian**: Ingests, tags, and connects disparate information into a semantic knowledge graph.
- **The Broadcaster**: Manages distribution channels, formatting content for specific platforms (TikTok, Twitter/X) without my intervention.
- **The Alchemist**: A creative partner that proposes permutations of existing ideas.

### The Impact
The Director allows me to operate with the output of a 5-person agency while maintaining the agility of a solo founder.
It turns "maintenance" into software, so I can focus on "direction."
