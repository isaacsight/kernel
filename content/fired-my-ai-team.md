I asked my AI agents to critique their own work today. The result was brutal.

## The Audit

My "Social Media Team" wasn't a team. It was a group of interns with no boss.

1.  **Absent Leadership:** The Creative Director existed in code but wasn't checking the work.
2.  **Robotic Soul:** The "chill" AI voice sounded like a sleep meditation gone wrong.
3.  **Visual Boredom:** We were using "Ken Burns" effects like it was 2015.

## The Fix: Installing a Boss

Today, I changed the code. I didn't add more features; I added **governance**.

Now, the `TikTokWorkflow` *cannot* run without the `CreativeDirector`'s explicit approval. If a script sounds too "hype-y" or uses engagement bait like "smash that like button," it gets rejected immediately.

## What's Next?

I'm researching **Kinetic Typography** (moving text) to replace the static slideshows. I'm also looking into **Trend APIs** so my agents know what's actually happening in the world, rather than guessing.

We're moving from a content pipeline to a creative studio.
