# The DTFR Harness
**Phase 1 of the Studio OS Open Source Mission.**

The Agentic Evaluation & Observability Harness (DTFR) is an open-source protocol designed to solve the "Trust Gap" in AI-native engineering. While traditional software uses unit tests, agentic systems require a **Felt-Sense Evaluation** standard that captures the nuance of autonomous judgment.

---

## Core Concepts

### 1. The Felt-Sense Card
A metadata registry for an agent's "personality" and "judgment boundaries." Each agent (Operator, Alchemist, etc.) has a card that defines what excellence "feels" like for their specific role.

### 2. The Supervision Ratio
A metric tracking the balance between autonomous action and human intervention. We aim for high autonomy without sacrificing alignment.

### 3. The Felt Right Index (FRI)
A composite score that aggregates technical success, alignment with studio values, and human feedback into a single, observable trace.

## Why Open Source?
We believe that **observability should have zero gates.** For agentic systems to be truly sovereign and trusted, the tools used to evaluate them must be transparent, forkable, and held by the community.
