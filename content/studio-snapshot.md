## 🎯 Current Focus

> **"Turning the studio into a closed-loop system."**

I am currently focused on removing human glue from the "Idea -> Research -> Draft -> Publish" pipeline. The goal is not to remove *me*, but to remove the friction of moving context between tools.

*   **Shipping**: One small artifact per week.
*   **Building**: The "Publisher" agent to handle final distribution.
*   **Refining**: The "Evolution Loop" that connects research to content generation.
