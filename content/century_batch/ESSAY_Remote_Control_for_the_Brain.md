# Input vs. Control

Most mobile apps are about **Consumption** (Instagram) or **Input** (Notes).
The Studio Companion App is about **Control**.
It is a remote control for a supercomputer.

## The Interface of Control
*   **Big Buttons**: "Deploy." "Refactor." "Research."
*   **Status Lights**: Green for healthy. Red for intervention needed.
*   **The Terminal Window**: A collapsible view into the raw thought process of the machine, for when you need to see the matrix.

## The "Stop Button"
The most important button on any remote control is **STOP**.
When an autonomous agent goes rogue (e.g., starts rewriting the wrong files), you need a hard kill switch.
Our mobile app has a "Panic Button" that instantly kills all server threads.
It is the emergency brake for the AI.

## Managing the External Brain
This app is the dashboard for your external cortex.
It shows you what your "Second Brain" is thinking about.
"Processing 3 background tasks."
It gives you a sense of mastery over the swarm. You are the pilot.
