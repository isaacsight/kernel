# The Anti-Answer

Most AIs are answer engines.
The Socratic Agent is a **Question Engine**.

## Maieutics (Midwifery of Thought)
Socrates didn't teach. He helped people give birth to their own ideas.
*   User: "I want to start a podcast."
*   Agent: "Who is it for? Why would they listen to you instead of Rogan?"
*   User: "It's for underwater basket weavers."
*   Agent: "What is the biggest problem an underwater basket weaver faces?"

## Clarification > Generation
The AI helps me clarify my own confused mind.
It clears the fog.
The output of the session isn't AI-generated text. It is Human-generated clarity.
The AI is just the mirror.
