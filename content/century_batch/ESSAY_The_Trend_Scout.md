# Hunting While You Sleep

Inspiration is usually serendipitous. You get lucky and see a tweet.
We industrialized Serendipity.

## The Scout Agent
The **Trend Scout** runs 24/7.
It monitors:
*   ArXiv (for new papers).
*   Twitter/X (for discourse).
*   ProductHunt (for launches).
*   HackerNews (for vibe).

## Filtering for "Novelty"
It doesn't just show me "The Top News." It looks for **Novelty**.
"Is this unexpected?"
"Does this contradict our current beliefs?"
It surfaces 5 "Sparks" every morning.

## Connecting the Dots
It attempts to link the new trend to my current drafts.
"You are writing about 'Agency.' Here is a new paper on 'Agentic Transformers' released today."
It is an automated relevance engine.
It ensures I am never late to the future.
