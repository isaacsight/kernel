# The Red Pen

Generative AI is famous for *creating* (images, text).
But its superpower is *destroying* (editing, critiquing, refining).
It is easier to verify than to generate.

## The Devil's Advocate
We use a **Contrarian Agent**.
Whatever opinion I hold, its job is to hold the opposite.
*   Me: "I think we should use React."
*   Agent: "Here are 3 reasons why React is overkill and Vanilla JS is better."
This forces me to sharpen my arguments. It prevents groupthink in a team of one.

## Design Audits
Before we ship, the **Inspector** runs a checklist:
1.  Are alt tags present?
2.  is the color contrast Accessible (WCAG AA)?
3.  Is the tone consistent?

It catches the stupid mistakes so I can focus on the smart ones.
We use AI to raise our baseline competence.
