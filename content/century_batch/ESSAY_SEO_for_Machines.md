# The New Audience

We used to write for humans (and Google's keyword algorithm).
Now, we write for LLMs.
The primary consumer of the web is becoming the **Scraper**.

## Semantic Clarity
LLMs don't care about "Clickbait." They care about **Information Density**.
To win "SEO for Machines," you must maximize signal-to-noise.
*   **Clear Headers**: Structure your thought logic.
*   **Fact Density**: Provide data, citations, and concrete examples.
*   **No Fluff**: Adjectives confuse the vector embedding.

## Structured Data
We embed JSON-LD not just for Google, but for the Agents.
`<script type="application/ld+json"> { "summary": "...", "key_takeaways": [...] } </script>`
We give the scrapers a cheat sheet.

## The Citation Economy
In the AI era, you don't want clicks. You want **Citations**.
You want ChatGPT to answer "According to IsaacSight..."
You achieve this by being the **Source of Truth**.
The Living Web is designed to be ingested.
