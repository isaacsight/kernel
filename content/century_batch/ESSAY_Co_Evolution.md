# We Shape Our Tools

Marshall McLuhan said, "We shape our tools, and thereafter our tools shape us."
This has never been truer than with AI.

## How We Change the Model
*   **Fine-tuning**: We feed it our emails, our code. It starts to sound like us.
*   **Prompting**: We learn the specific incantations that unlock its genius.

## How the Model Changes Us
*   **Syntax Agnosticism**: I have stopped caring about syntax. I don't remember how to write a Python `for` loop from scratch. I just know the logic.
*   **Architectural Thinking**: I think in systems now, not lines of code.
*   **Speed**: My patience has evaporated. If I can't build it in 1 hour, I'm bored.

## The New Species
We are becoming a hybrid species.
The "Unaided Human" is becoming obsolete in the digital economy.
The "Symbiont" (Human + Swarm) is the new baseline.
We are not just using AI. We are merging with it.
