# Scaling Quality Control

In a content factory, quality usually drops as volume rises.
With the **Editor Agent**, we inverse this law.
We can audit *every* sentence of *every* post.

## The Checklist
The Editor runs a 50-point inspection on every draft:
*   Passive voice check.
*   Cliche detection ("Game-changer", "Unlocking").
*   Logical flow analysis.
*   Tone consistency.

## The "Edit Button"
I don't just press "Publish." I press "Audit."
The specific, actionable feedback I get ("Paragraph 3 is weak") makes me a better writer.
It is like having a stern English teacher looking over my shoulder 24/7.

## Consistency at Scale
When you have 100 posts (like this Century Batch), consistency is hard.
The AI ensures that Essay #1 and Essay #100 sound like they were written by the same person on the same day.
It is the glue that holds the anthology together.
