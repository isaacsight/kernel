# Flaws are Features

When AI makes a mistake—a six-fingered hand, a hallucinated library—our instinct is to hide it.
We scrub the output to make it look "Human."
This is dishonesty.

## The Glitch Sets Us Free
We embrace the **Neural Glitch**.
If the AI generates a weird metaphor, keep it.
If the CSS generator makes a layout that breaks the grid, publish it.
These artifacts define the aesthetic of this era. They are the "grain" of the medium.

## Evidence of Intelligence
A perfect, sterilized output looks like a template.
A weird, slightly broken output looks like *computation*.
It proves that work was done.
We show the logs. We show the reasoning trace. We show the errors.

## The Raw Aesthetic
Our site `doesthisfeelright.com` is designed to feel "Under Construction."
Because Intelligence is never finished.
The glitch is the reminder that the system is trying, failing, and evolving.
