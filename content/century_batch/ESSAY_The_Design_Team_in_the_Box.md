# Scaling the Creative Director

Every freelancer dreams of having a team. A researcher, a designer, a copywriter.
The Studio OS provides this team, not as employees, but as **Micro-Services**.

## The Persona Pattern
We don't just ask "AI" for help. We ask:
*   **The Typography Nerd**: obsessing over kerning.
*   **The Grid Master**: enforcing alignment.
*   **The Brand Policeman**: ensuring tone consistency.

## The Council of Critics
When I make a design, I submit it to the **Council**.
*   **Critique**: "The contrast is too low." "The hierarchy is confused."
*   **Defense**: I argue back. "It's a stylistic choice."
*   **Synthesis**: We agree on a middle ground.

## Better than Yes-Men
A human junior employee might be afraid to critique the boss.
The AI has no fear. It will tell you your design is ugly.
This radical honesty is what makes it a true consultant.
It raises the floor of quality for every project.
