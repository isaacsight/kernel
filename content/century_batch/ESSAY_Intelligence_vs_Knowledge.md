# Rote vs Reasoning

Wikipedia has all the Knowledge. It has zero Intelligence.
A calculator has zero Knowledge. It has (very narrow) Intelligence (processing capability).

## The Library and the Librarian
*   **Knowledge** is the Vector DB (The Library).
*   **Intelligence** is the Agent (The Librarian).

## The Mix
We often confuse the two. We think GPT-4 is smart because it knows the capital of France.
That's just memory.
It is smart because it can write a poem about the capital of France in the style of Eminem.
That is **Processing**.

## Future Proofing
Knowledge becomes obsolete (APIs change).
Intelligence (Reasoning capability) is durable.
We invest in the Librarian, not the books.
The books can be swapped out. The ability to read them is what matters.
