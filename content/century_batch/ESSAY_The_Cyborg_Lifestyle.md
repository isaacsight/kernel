# Waking Up Integrated

The alarm doesn't ring. The lights fade up because the **Sleep Agent** detected my REM cycle ended.
"Good morning, Isaac. While you slept, I merged 3 PRs and declined 2 meetings."

## The Integrated Breakfast
I don't scroll Twitter. I scroll my **Context Feed**.
*   Section 1: "System Health" (Green).
*   Section 2: "Global News" (Summarized by relevance to my projects).
*   Section 3: "Ideas" (Generated during the night by the **Dream Agent**).

## Work without Friction
I sit at the desk. I don't "open files."
I say, "Let's work on the payment flow."
The IDE snaps to the right context. The documentation opens. The relevant agents wake up.
I am not fighting the computer. I am dancing with it.

## The Evening Handoff
At 6 PM, I don't "clock out." I "hand over."
"Researcher, keep looking for papers on liquid neural networks. Architect, refactor the user model."
I walk away. The system keeps working.
This is not using a tool. This is living with a partner.
