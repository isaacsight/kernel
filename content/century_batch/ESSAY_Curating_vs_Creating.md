# The Director's Chair

The Painter mixes paint. The Director chooses the shot.
AI moves the artist from the Canvas to the Chair.

## Selection as Art
When you generate 100 images and pick 1, that choice is the artistic act.
Your "Taste" is the filter.
Two people using Midjourney get different results because they choose differently.
The tool is uniform. The taste is unique.

## The Collage Era
We are entering the Golden Age of Collage.
Taking a code snippet from here, an image from there, a text block from here.
Stitching them into a coherent whole.
The skill is "Synthesis."
Seeing the big picture is more important than crafting the pixel.

## Protecting the Soul
The risk is losing the soul in the automation.
The Artist's job is to inject the **Human Error**, the **Biographical Detail**, the **Opinion** that the machine cannot fake.
We provide the Soul. The machine provides the Body.
