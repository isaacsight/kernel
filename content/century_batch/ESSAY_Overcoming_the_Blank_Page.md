# The Terror of White Space

The hardest part of writing is the first sentence.
The **Seed Generator** ensures I never face a blank page.

## The Prompt: "Start Me Off"
I sit down. I have a vague topic: "AI and Gardening."
I hit the Seed Button.
The AI generates:
1.  A provocative opening question.
2.  Three potential headlines.
3.  A bulleted outline.

## Reacting > Inventing
I might hate all 3 headlines.
"No, that's wrong. It's not about plants, it's about soil."
**Boom.** I have started writing.
I am reacting to the bad seed.
The specific quality of the seed doesn't matter. Its existence breaks the paralysis.

## The Muse in the Machine
The Seed Generator is the Muse.
It whispers the initial idea.
You do the work.
But you calculate the friction of "Starting" down to zero.
