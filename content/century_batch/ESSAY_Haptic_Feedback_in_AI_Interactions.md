# Feeling the Machine

Text is visual. But presence is physical.
We use the Taptic Engine to give the AI physical weight.

## The Vocabulary of Vibration
*   **Success**: A crisp, sharp click. (Like locking a door).
*   **Thinking**: A subtle, heartbeat-like thrumming. (Like a purring cat).
*   **Error**: A double heavy buzz. (Like a warning track).
*   **Typing**: A light tick-tick-tick as the stream arrives.

## Emotional Bandwidth
Haptics convey emotion faster than text.
When you feel the "Thinking" heartbeat, you know the agent is engaged. You don't need to stare at the screen. You can hold the phone in your pocket and *feel* the work happening.

## Accessibility
For users with visual impairments, haptics provide a secondary channel of information.
"Did the deploy work?" The crisp click says yes.
It makes the virtual feel tangible.
