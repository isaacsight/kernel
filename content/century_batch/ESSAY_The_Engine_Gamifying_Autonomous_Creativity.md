# XP for Shipping

Creativity is hard work. It's easy to procrastinate.
We borrowed a trick from video games: **The Dopamine Loop**.
The Studio OS tracks your creative output like a RPG.

## The Achievement Tracker
*   **"Graphomaniac"**: Write 1,000 words in a day.
*   **"Ship It"**: Deploy to production on a Friday.
*   **"Clean Code"**: Refactor 5 files.

These aren't just badges. They unlock system privileges.
(e.g., Unlocking the "GPT-5 Access" requires 10,000 XP).

## Visualizing Progress
We show a "GitHub Contribution Graph" for everything, not just code.
Writing. Design. Ideation.
Seeing the "Green Squares" of productivity motivates you to do *something* every day.

## The Daily Quest
Every morning, the **Coach Agent** assigns a Daily Quest.
"Today's Quest: Fix the navigation bug."
It turns a chore into a mission.
And when you complete it, the screen explodes in confetti.
It sounds silly. It works.
