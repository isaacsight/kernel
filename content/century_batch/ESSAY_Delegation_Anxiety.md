# The Micromanager's Recovery

The biggest barrier to adopting Agency is not technology. It is **Anxiety**.
"If I don't write the code, how do I know it works?"
"If I don't see the cursor move, is it happening?"

## The Comfort of the Command Line
We cling to the CLI because it gives us the illusion of total control.
We execute one line at a time.
Agency requires a leap of faith. "Fix the database."

## Gradual Release of Control
We solved this with **Permission Tiers**.
1.  **Level 1**: AI proposes, Human executes. (Zero risk).
2.  **Level 2**: AI executes in Sandbox. Human reviews.
3.  **Level 3**: AI executes in Staging.
4.  **Level 4**: AI deploys to Prod.

You don't start at Level 4. You earn your way there.
The anxiety fades as the track record grows.
