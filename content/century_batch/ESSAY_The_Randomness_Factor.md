# Controlled Chaos

In LLMs, `temperature` controls randomness.
*   0.0 = Deterministic. Boring. Safe.
*   1.0 = Chaotic. Creative. Crazy.

## Dialing the Madness
We use high temperature (0.9) for brainstorming.
"Give me 10 wild ideas for a mobile app."
(Result: "An app that screams if you drop it.") -> Stupid, but funny.
We use low temperature (0.2) for coding.
"Write a binary search."
(Result: Perfect code).

## Breaking local Maxima
Humans get stuck in "Local Maxima." We think in ruts.
High-temp AI is a "Rut Breaker."
It throws a random mutation into the thought process.
It forces lateral thinking.

## The Digital Aleatoric
Artists like Brian Eno used "Oblique Strategies" cards to introduce randomness.
AI is the ultimate Oblique Strategy.
It introduces the Noise that allows the Signal to change.
Creativity needs chaos. The prompt adds the order.
