# Dreaming in Images

Text is not enough for design. We need visuals.
The **Moodboard Agent** connects the concept to the aesthetic.

## Semantic to Visual
1.  **Concept**: "Cyberpunk minimalist gardening."
2.  **Expansion**: Agent expands this to keywords: "Neon green, concrete, fern, brutalism."
3.  **Generation**: Agent calls DALL-E 3 / Midjourney to generate textures and layouts.

## The Curated Feed
Instead of scrolling Pinterest, the agent builds a custom Pinterest board for *this specific project*.
It saves me hours of searching.
"Here are 20 textures that match your vibe."

## Breaking Art Block
The goal is not to use the generated images directly (they often have artifacts).
The goal is to break the "White Canvas" paralysis.
It puts *something* on the screen.
It is easier to critique a bad image than to invent a good one from zero.
