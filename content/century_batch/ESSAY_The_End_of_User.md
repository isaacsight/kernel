# Moving Up the Stack

The term "User" implies consumption. You use a toaster. You use an app. You are the endpoint.

With the Studio OS, you are no longer the endpoint. You are the **Origin**.
You are the **Operator**.

## The Definition of an Operator
An operator manages a system that runs itself.
*   **The User** clicks "Play" on Spotify.
*   **The Operator** sets the parameters for the DJ algorithm.

*   **The User** writes a function.
*   **The Operator** defines the architectural constraints and lets the Swarm implement the functions.

## The Skill Shift
The skills required to be a good Operator are different:
1.  **System Thinking**: Understanding how the agents interact.
2.  **Constraint Setting**: Defining the boundaries of the playground.
3.  **Taste**: Recognizing what is "good" when you didn't make it yourself.

## Promotion
AI didn't take your job. It promoted you.
You used to be the bricklayer. Now you are the Foreman.
The question is: Are you ready to stop touching the bricks?
