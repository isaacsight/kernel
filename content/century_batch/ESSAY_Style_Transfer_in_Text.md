# Trying on Voices

We accept filters in Instagram. "Make this photo look Vintage."
Why not filters for text?
"Make this email look Professional."
"Make this blog post look like Hemingway."

## The Style Gan for Words
The Studio OS allows **Style Transfer**.
I write a messy, rambling draft.
I apply the "Hemingway Filter."
*   Adjectives deleted.
*   Sentences shortened.
*   Active voice enforced.

## Learning via Mimicry
Seeing my own thoughts rephrased by a master style teaches me.
I see *how* the economy of words works.
It is an interactive writing lesson.

## The Polyglot Brand
We can adapt our message to the channel.
*   LinkedIn: Professional, enthusiastic.
*   Twitter: Short, cynical, lowercase.
*   Whitepaper: Academic, rigorous.
Same core content. Different render settings.
The message remains; the medium adapts.
