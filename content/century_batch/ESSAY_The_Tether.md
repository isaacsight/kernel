# The Server in Your Pocket

Your phone is not powerful enough to run a Swarm of Agentic LLMs.
But it is powerful enough to **control** them.
We built **The Tether**: A secure, low-latency bridge between the mobile client and the Studio Server.

## The Architecture of the Tether
*   **Websockets**: Constant, bi-directional data stream. No polling.
*   **State syncing**: The "Active Agent" on the server is mirrored on the phone.
*   **Authentication**: Biometric auth on the phone signs the packets sent to the server.

## Agency at the Edge
The Tether allows you to take the **Council** with you.
You are at a dinner party. You have an idea. You whisper it into the phone.
The audio is streamed to the Server. The Server transcribes it, the **Trend Scout** analyzes it, and the **Writer** drafts a stub.
By the time you get home, the work is done.

## The Psychological Shift
This breaks the "Work/Life" boundary in a way that needs careful management.
But it also breaks the "Desk/World" boundary.
You can be productive while walking in the woods. The phone is just the microphone; the heavy lifting happens in the cloud.
