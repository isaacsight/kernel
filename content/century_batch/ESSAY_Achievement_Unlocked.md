# Making Bugs Fun

Fixing bugs is depressing.
Unless it's a game.

## The Bounty System
The **Guardian Agent** places a "Bounty" on bugs.
*   "Critical Crash: 500 Gold."
*   "Typo: 10 Gold."
I collect Gold to "buy" hours of automated agent time.
"I'll trade 1000 Gold to have the Researcher summarize this book for me."

## The Leaderboard (of One)
I compete against my past self.
"Last week, you shipped 4 features. Can you beat it?"
The system provides the "Ghost Car" from Mario Kart.
It pushes you to optimize your velocity.

## Flow State Triggers
Games are good at inducing Flow because the detailed feedback is instant.
We added "Combo Meters" to the IDE.
If you type without backspacing for 5 minutes, you enter "Streak Mode." The UI glows.
It gamifies the act of typing itself.
