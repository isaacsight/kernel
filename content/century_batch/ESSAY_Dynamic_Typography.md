# Type that Feels

Variable Fonts allow us to animate weight, slant, and width.
Why is this usually controlled by the mouse?
It should be controlled by the **Meaning**.

## Sentiment-Driven Weight
We built a prototype where the font weight increases with the "Urgency" of the text.
*   "This is a minor point." (Light, 300)
*   **"THIS IS CRITICAL."** (Bold, 800)
The text shouts or whispers based on its semantic vector.

## Reading Speed Optimization
The AI analyzes the "complexity" of a paragraph.
*   **Dense technical text**: Increases line-height and letter-spacing to aid comprehension.
*   **Narrative flow**: Tightens spacing to encourage speed reading.
The typography adapts to the *cognitive load* of the reader.

## The Living Ink
This turns text from dead pixels into living ink.
It treats reading not as scanning, but as an interaction.
The words reach out to you.
