# The One-Person Unicorn

Agencies exist to aggregate talent. You need a writer, a designer, a dev.
What happens when one person has access to all those talents via API?

## The Collapse of the Mid-Market
Large agencies will survive (for large clients).
Freelancers will thrive (using AI tools).
The middle—the 20-person shop charging for "production"—will die.
The AI is the production department.

## The Rise of the Auteur
We are returning to the era of the Auteur.
A single creative vision, executed by a fleet of machines.
The Studio OS is the cockpit for this Auteur.
It amplifies the individual leverage to the point where one person can ship a system that used to require a department.

## The Service is Strategy
The only thing you can sell is **Insight**.
The execution is trending toward free.
Consultancies must stop selling "Hours of coding" and start selling "Years of wisdom."
