# The Synthetic Focus Group

User testing is slow and expensive.
Before we show humans, we show the **Simulacra**.

## Persona Bots
We spin up agents with specific biases:
*   **The Boomer**: "I don't understand this UI. text is too small."
*   **The Zoomer**: "This feels dated. Needs more motion."
*   **The Power User**: "Where are the keyboard shortcuts?"

## The Firing Squad
We present the design to 5 personas simultaneously.
They roast it.
"This navigation flow makes no sense."
"Why is the signup button hidden?"

## Pre-Validation
This doesn't replace human testing. But it filters out the obvious failures.
By the time a human sees it, the design has already survived the gauntlet.
It is robust.
