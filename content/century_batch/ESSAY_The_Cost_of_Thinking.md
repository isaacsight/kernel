# Thought isn't Free

In System 1 (ChatGPT free tier), thought is cheap.
In System 2 (Agent Swarms), thought is expensive.
A single complex task might consume 500k tokens ($5.00).

## The CFO Agent
We literally have a **Budget Agent**.
It watches the token burn rate.
If a task is spiraling ("The Researcher has browsed 500 pages"), it cuts the cord.
"This insight is not worth $50."

## ROI of Intelligence
We must start thinking about the **Return on Intelligence**.
*   Spending $10 to fix a critical bug? High ROI.
*   Spending $10 to generate a haiku? Low ROI.

## The Energy Audit
It's not just dollars. It's energy.
Training and running these models burns carbon.
We have a moral obligation to be efficient with our compute.
Do not ask the supercomputer to add 2+2.
