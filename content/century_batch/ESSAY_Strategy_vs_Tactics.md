# The Horizon Line

AI is a Tactician. It is excellent at "How."
*   "How do I center a div?"
*   "How do I optimize this query?"

It is terrible at "What" and "Why."
*   "What product should I build?"
*   "Why does this market matter?"

## The Alignment Problem
AI optimizes for the probable. Strategy optimizes for the *possible* but *unlikely*.
Great strategy often looks like a bad idea at first.
An LLM will talk you out of a contrarian bet because it is trained on the consensus.

## The Human CEO
This clarifies the human role.
We are the **Strategists**. We pick the mountain.
The AI is the **Sherpa**. It carries the pack and finds the path.
If you ask the Sherpa which mountain to climb, it will pick the smallest one.
Don't abdicate the "Why."
