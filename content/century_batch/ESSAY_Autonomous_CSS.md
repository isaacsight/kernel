# The Self-Styling Interface

We treat content as dynamic, but design as static.
Why?
Why can't the padding change? Why can't the color palette shift based on the sentiment of the text?

## Semantic Styling
We are experimenting with **Sentiment-Aware CSS**.
*   **Serious Content**: The site shifts to Serif fonts, high contrast, monochrome.
*   **Playful Content**: The site shifts to Sans-Serif, looser spacing, vibrant accent colors.

## Chaos vs Cohesion
The risk of autonomous design is ugliness.
We solve this with **Design Tokens**.
The AI cannot pick *any* color. It can only pick from the `Brand Palette`.
It cannot pick *any* font size. It must follow the `Type Scale`.
This gives the AI freedom within a cage.

## A/B Testing on Autopilot
The system runs micro-experiments.
"Does increasing the line-height by 2px improve reading time?"
It tries it for 10% of traffic. If it works, it commits the change to `main`.
The site optimizes itself while we sleep.
