# Reasoning from the Atom

LLMs operate by Analogy (training data).
"Most websites have a navbar, so I will add a navbar."
This is Cargo Cult design.

## The Physics Engine of Thought
We prompt the model to strip away assumptions.
"Constraint: Assume no standard UI elements. What is the most efficient way to navigate?"
Result: A gesture-based interface.

## Escaping the Average
Training data is the average of all human knowledge.
To be exceptional, you must defy the average.
First Principles prompts force the model out of the "Probabilistic Center" and into the "High-Utility Outliers."
It stops copying and starts inventing.
