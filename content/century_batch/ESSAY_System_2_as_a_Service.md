# Fast Thinking, Slow Thinking

Daniel Kahneman taught us that humans have two modes of thought:
*   **System 1**: Fast, instinctive, emotional. (2+2=4)
*   **System 2**: Slow, deliberative, logical. (17 x 24 = ?)

Most Chatbots are **System 1**. They predict the next token immediately. They don't "stop and think."

## The Studio OS Brain
We are building **System 2 as a Service**.
When you ask the Studio a hard question, it shouldn't answer in 200 milliseconds. It should say:
*"I need to think about this. Give me 10 minutes."*

## The Privilege of Latency
In an instant-gratification world, asking for time is a power move.
Deep Reasoning agents (like the **Architect**) take that time. They:
1.  Draft a plan.
2.  Critique the plan.
3.  Simulate the outcome.
4.  Refine.

## Renting Genius
You don't need to be a genius every minute of the day. You just need to have one on speed dial.
By offloading high-complexity reasoning to the Swarm, you free up your own System 1 to be creative, intuitive, and flowing.
Let the machine do the hard math. You do the jazz.
