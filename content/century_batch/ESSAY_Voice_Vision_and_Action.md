# Beyond the Keyboard

Typing is slow. Speaking is fast. Pointing is faster.
The future interface combines all three.

## Voice as the Commander
Voice is for Intent. "Make this look like the 90s."
It captures the high-level goal in a second.

## Vision as the Context
The Camera (or Screen Context) is for Grounding.
The AI "sees" what you are looking at. You don't copy-paste code. You just point.
"Fix *this* error."

## Action as the Output
The AI doesn't reply. It acts.
It changes the CSS. It runs the script.
The loop becomes:
**See (AI) -> Hear (AI) -> Do (AI)**.

## The Frictionless Loop
When you remove the friction of typing and reading, the speed of iteration explodes.
You can iterate on a design 50 times in a minute just by talking and pointing.
This is the velocity of thought.
