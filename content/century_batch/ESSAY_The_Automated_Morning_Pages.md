# The Journal that Writes Back

"Morning Pages" (Julia Cameron) is the practice of writing 3 pages of stream-of-consciousness every morning to clear the mind.
I automated it.

## The Interview
Instead of staring at a notebook, the **Therapist Agent** interviews me.
"How are you feeling about the launch?"
"What is blocking you?"
I dictate the answers.

## The Mirror
The Agent then reflects it back.
"It sounds like you are anxious about the server load, but confident in the code. Is that right?"
This mirroring provides clarity faster than aimless writing.

## Emotional Telemetry
We track the sentiment over time.
"You have been 20% more anxious this week than last week."
This data is actionable. "Maybe I need a break."
It is self-awareness as a service.
