# The Friends We Outgrow

Hello there, my dear reader,

Today, I'd like to share some thoughts about a subject that I believe many of us have encountered: the friends we outgrow. This is a delicate matter, one that touches upon our hearts and helps us understand the dynamic nature of our relationships.

## The Unwritten Contract

As children, we make unspoken promises with our friends. These pacts are not about shared possessions or mutual favors but about shared experiences, emotions, and growth. We embark on this journey together, navigating life's ups and downs side by side. But sometimes, as we grow, these friendships shift, evolve, or even dissipate.

## The Shifts

There are many reasons why friendships change. Some shifts are due to geographical distance, others to differing life paths, and still more to personal growth. As we evolve, so do our needs and expectations from our relationships. Sometimes, the people who once filled our lives no longer fit into the new mold we've created for ourselves.

## The Lessons

Change can be difficult, but it also offers valuable lessons. In recognizing that some friendships have run their course, we learn to accept change and adapt. We gain a deeper understanding of ourselves and our needs, and we come to appreciate the fleeting nature of life's connections.

## The Future

As we continue to grow and evolve, it's essential to remember that our relationships will also change. Some friendships will endure, while others will fade away. But just as new friendships blossom, we learn to let go, cherishing the memories of the ones that have moved on.

Does this feel true?
