# Product Ecosystem

The machines we build inside the Studio are designed to be **observable, forkable, and verifiable**. We aren't just shipping tools; we are defining the **opinionated OS layer** that turns raw model power into coherent, governable systems.

---

## 1. **Studio OS as a Protocol**
Instead of a closed product, we are building Studio OS as an **open protocol for agent governance**. It is a specification for how agents declare capabilities, how decisions get logged immutably, and how guardrails are enforced across any stack.
**The Push**: We're publishing the spec and shipping a reference implementation on GitHub, allowing enterprises to fork the OS and maintain total autonomy.

---

## 2. **Autonomous System Designer**
A dev tool for the agentic era. It ingests your codebase, documentation, and team structure, then **generates** a minimal Studio OS (agents, workflows, metrics) specifically tailored to your technical ecosystem.
**The Push**: Use this as a diagnostic runner on your repo to receive a “system health + OS prescription” report. It’s AI engineering, not consulting.

---

## 3. **Agentic OS Bootcamp (Open Curriculum)**
A self-paced, fully free intensive where engineers and product leads **build** their own Studio OS. We provide the curriculum, the patterns, and the "felt-quality" benchmarks. Participants leave with a running, production-ready OS, powered by our open codebase.
**The Push**: This builds a global community of AI-native engineering leads qualified to deploy and govern agentic systems at scale.

---

## 4. **Frontier Team Blueprints**
Don’t just take advice—**deploy** a Frontier Team. We provide the specialized blueprints for Architect, Librarian, and Operator agents that you can run inside your own GitHub or Slack.
**The Push**: We open-source the "Judgment Patterns" of our internal agents, allowing you to run a specialized pod in your own environment for free.

---

## 5. **Decision Token / On-Chain OS**
Every decision, manual override, and “felt wrong” moment is logged as a **verifiable credential**. Over time, you build a blockchain of judgment that proves your system’s reliability and decision-making history.
**The Push**: In partnership with crypto-native AI platforms, we are building the first on-chain agentic OS for trustless verification of agent actions.

---

## 6. **AI Engineering Research Lab**
The Studio is a public lab for reproducible experiments. We publish findings on model performance, latency, error rates, and alignment drift within agentic loops.
**The Push**: This acts as a benchmarking hub for AI engineering, attracting research partnerships that need real-world agentic data.

---

## 7. **Hardware OS: Frontier Console**
The **Frontier Console** isn’t just a display—it’s a distributed edge device. It runs agent inference locally, logs decisions offline, and syncs when connected, designed for regulated industries that cannot rely on cloud-only APIs.
**The Push**: Total privacy, e-ink status dashboards, and local autonomy for healthcare, defense, and high-security engineering teams.

---

## 8. **Studio OS Copilot (Universal Interface)**
The **Studio OS Copilot** is a simple, universal decision-capture and agent-governance layer that plugs into every surface you work in (browser, phone, IDE, hardware). It asks **“Does this feel right?”** and learns from your manual overrides and judgment signals. It turns AI from a chatbot into a living operating system that proves its reliability with a verifiable decision ledger.
**The Push**: [Explore the Strategy & Positioning →](./strategy.html) | [Explore the Copilot Ecosystem →](./extensions.html)

---

> [!TIP]
> Each of these products is designed to make the agentic layer **visible and verifiable**, ensuring that as systems grow more complex, they remain fundamentally "right."
