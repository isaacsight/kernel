## Cut mix (5 cuts)

An interview issue calls for: 1 drop + 2 interview clips + 1
pull quote + 1 masthead note. The cover lives as the drop; the
interview's two best answers become clips; one standalone line
becomes a pull quote; one masthead note explains why the
interview is the right tool for a craft issue.

| # | Cut | File | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Issue drop | `drop.md` | 14s |

**Total upload:** 5 cuts over ~8 days.
