Antigravity Studio

Systems
{{ post_context }}

The Mission

Antigravity is a creative technical studio. We do not sell hours. We do not do "client work" in the traditional sense. We build ownership.

The Model

The studio operates on a Venture Studio model, but for uncommon software. We identify problems that are too small for venture capital but too complex for indie hackers. We build the solution, operationalize it, and then decide: 1. Spin out: Hire a CEO and raise capital. 2. Hold: Keep as a cash-flowing asset. 3. Kill: Extract the lessons and move on.

The Portfolio

Current active experiments include:

  • Kokoro-TTS: High-fidelity AI voice synthesis infrastructure.
  • The Director: Autonomous agent orchestration.
  • Does This Feel Right?: Signals and philosophy.
