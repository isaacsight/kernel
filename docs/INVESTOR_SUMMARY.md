## Core Innovation: Dual-Capital Thesis

Every asset has **two parallel funding sources**: TradFi + DeFi

| Source | Rate | Settlement | Compliance |
|--------|------|------------|------------|
| TradFi (Banks) | 7-9% | Days | Manual |
| DeFi (Protocols) | 3-4% | Instant | Programmable |
| **Spread** | **3-5%** | - | - |

This spread is **structural, not temporary**. Different regulatory frameworks, settlement speeds, and risk appetites create persistent arbitrage.

### The 60% Rule
Optimal allocation: 60% DeFi capital + 40% TradFi capital
- Regulatory safe harbor
- Lower blended cost of capital
- Instant settlement for majority of flow
- 70% compliance cost reduction via smart contracts
