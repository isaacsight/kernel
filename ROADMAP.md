# Studio OS: Open Source Roadmap (2026)

The Studio OS is evolving from a private development environment into a **fully free and open-source platform** for **Agentic Systems Engineering (ASE)**. We are preserving our integrity by opening the entire engine—making our tools, patterns, and philosophy accessible to all.

## Strategic Objectives (The Commons)
- **Everything is Free**: We believe the future of AI trust should not be gated. All core tools, agents, and systems are open and free.
- **Integrity through Transparency**: Maintaining our "Zen Architect" standards by making our internal codebase the public reference implementation.

## Core Vision: Collaborative Trust
DTFR reframes quality from technical measurement to a **collaborative practice** where designers and engineers jointly encode what "feels right."

## 🟢 Phase 1: The Evaluation Foundation (Current)
*Target: Q1 2026*
- **[NEW] "Does This Feel Right?" (DTFR) Harness**: A lightweight instrumentation and evaluation package for agentic systems.
- **Emergent Intelligence**: A distributed cognitive system optimizing the global trust-space.
- **Critical Trust Infrastructure**: Positioning DTFR as the "Red Hat of Agent Evaluation" for the 2026 market shift.
- **Knowledge Commons**: Shared evaluation protocols to prevent vendor capture.
- **Compliance Foundation**: Built-in observability for human-in-the-loop regulatory requirements.
- **Reference Implementation**: Formalizing the Studio OS core (the 1000-prompt library and multi-agent mission control) into the `studio-os` public repo.

## 🟡 Phase 2: Composable Kits
*Target: Q2 2026*
- **Does This Feel Right? Kits**: Small, composable packages for specific agentic behaviors:
    - `dtfr-eval-harness`: Observability & evaluation.
    - `dtfr-agent-patterns`: Reusable agent blueprints (The Alchemist, The Guardian).
    - `dtfr-ux-agent`: UI patterns for human-in-the-loop agents.
- **Integration Layer**: Bridging Studio OS kits with LangChain, CrewAI, and Microsoft Autogen.

## 🔴 Phase 3: The Evolutionary Marketplace
*Target: H2 2026*
- **Agentic Marketplace**: A community-driven repository of "Blessed Patterns" (prompts, tool-use strategies, and evaluation criteria).
- **Custom Studio Deployments**: Tools for companies to "Fork the Lab" and deploy private instances of the Studio OS ecosystem.

---

## Strategic Guardrails
- **Opinionated over Generic**: We will not compete with infra-only platforms. We focus on the *human-AI interface*.
- **Directing over Typing**: Every tool we build must simplify the act of *Directing* complex systems rather than just *Typing* more code.
- **Human-in-the-Loop**: "Felt sense" remains a first-class citizen in every evaluation trace.
