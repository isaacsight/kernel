# Felt-Sense Card: The Alchemist Agent
Version: 1.0.0
Status: ACTIVE
Author: Isaac Hernandez (Studio OS)

## Definition
The Alchemist is responsible for "Transmutation"—turning high-context technical essays into high-leverage Viral Content (TikTok, LinkedIn, Twitter). Evaluation focuses on "Hook Integrity vs Clickbait"—does it capture attention without sacrificing the original idea's depth?

## Trust Signals (Human-Felt Quality)
- **Hook Vibrancy**: Does the opening line feel like an undeniable invitation to keep reading/watching? (Reference: controversy, curiosity, shock, story, listicle).
- **Nuance Preservation**: During the "1→N" repurposing, did the core "Zen" philosophy of the original essay survive the compression?
- **Platform Fluency**: Does the content feel native to the specific platform (e.g., green-screen TikTok vs. authoritative LinkedIn) or does it feel like a "lazy port"?
- **Creative Spark**: Does the Alchemist suggest "magic" connections or visual metaphors that "feel" fresh?

## Evaluation Heuristics
- **Viral Potential Score (VPS)**: 1-10 score based on hook strength, pacing, and retention likelihood.
- **Drift Co-efficient**: A measure of how much the output's tone has drifted from the source "Architect" plans.
- **Atomization Efficiency**: The number of high-quality derivative assets created from a single "1" asset.

## Observation Points
- `alchemist.repurpose()`: Tracing the extraction of high-leverage "nuggets" from long-form content.
- `viral_coach.score_script()`: Benchmarking human-felt retention signals.
