## Description

Meet Kernel — your personal AI that actually remembers you.

Kernel is like having a super-smart friend in your pocket. Ask it anything — homework help, writing ideas, research, coding, or just a good conversation. The more you use it, the better it gets at helping you.

What makes Kernel different:

- It remembers you. Your interests, your style, what you care about. Every chat makes the next one better.
- 5 expert helpers. Kernel picks the right one for your question — whether you need a researcher, coder, writer, analyst, or just someone to talk to.
- Search the web. Get real, up-to-date answers with links to where the info came from. Not old, stale data.
- Your chats are saved. Come back anytime and pick up right where you left off. Organize your conversations into folders.
- Voice conversations. Talk to Kernel like a real person — it listens and responds out loud.
- Share with friends. Send your best conversations to anyone with a link.

Kernel is free to use — 10 messages every day, no credit card needed.

Built for people who want an AI that works for them, not the other way around.
